--> Problem Statement
Analyze retail sales data to identify revenue drivers, profit trends.

--> Key Insights
- Technology category generates highest revenue
- Furniture category has low profitability due to high discounts
- West region performs better in profit margins

---

--> Business Recommendations
- Reduce discounts in low-margin categories
- Focus on high-performing regions
- Optimize product mix for profitability
